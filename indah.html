Bubble tea Is also known as Taiwanese Tea, Boba Tea, or Pearl Tea.
A distinctive feature of Bubble tea is a variety of flavored tea drinks with additional tapioca pudding in the form of balls or other types of pudding.
Boba is a tapioca ball which is often filled with bubble tea. Boba is made from tapioca flour, which is flour from cassava.
Bubble Tea is a beverage made from tea, milk and boba.
Classic bubble tea usually uses black tea.
However, over the times and innovations they offer various types of tea, such as jasmine, green, oolong, matcha, fruit tea, earl gray, and others.
